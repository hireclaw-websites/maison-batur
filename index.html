Why here

Not a city bistro moved uphill — a French restaurant designed for Kintamani.

Maison Batur is built around place: highland produce, volcanic air, warm service, and a dining rhythm that starts with the view but stays memorable because of the food.

Inside, the mood is calm and open. Outside, the mountain does the talking. The menu brings the two together with polished French technique and a Bali sense of ease.

“Come for the volcano, stay for the sauce.”
